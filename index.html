Welcome to my academic profile. I am Zhou Zichen, a third-year undergraduate student at Beijing Institute of Technology, focusing on embodied intelligence, multimodal large language models, and computer vision research.

My research interests include developing intelligent embodied systems that can understand and interact with the physical world through multiple modalities. I have published research at the prestigious Robotics: Science and Systems (RSS) conference.
